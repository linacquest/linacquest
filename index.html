LiNAC Quest: Disrupting Radiotherapy Precision for Cancer Treatment

LiNAC Quest?

Is an exciting quest to save millions of lives! Based on an article published in the "International Congress of Medical Sciences (ICMS 2024)" - students and young doctors", it intends to concern LiNAC makers to upgrade their devices to use negative charges exclusively. Such LiNACs may tremendously increase the precision and safety of radiotherapy in cancer treatments, reducing side effects, to be demonstrated in clinical trials. The quest consist of three stages:

Article in page 29

Stage 1 (done)

Scopus article (Graphical Abstract) with the hypothesis of using negative charges in LiNAC devices that may increase the precision and reduce the side effects of cancer radiotherapy

Stage 2

Engage LiNAC manufacturers to upgrade their devices using negative charges, e.g., Varian Medical Systems (USA), Elekta AB (Sweden) and Sumitomo Heavy Industries (Japan) (list)

Stage 3

Engage medical centers to conduct clinical trials to demonstrate the paradigm-changing premises on increased precision and reduction of side effects in cancer patients
